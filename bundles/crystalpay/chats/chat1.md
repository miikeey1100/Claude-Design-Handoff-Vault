# CrystalPay — AI Payment Dashboard — Design Session

## User Intent

Build me an AI-powered payment dashboard with a premium glass aesthetic. Show my total balance as a hero card, quick action buttons for Send/Receive/Exchange/Invest, recent transaction history, an AI insight panel from Atlas spotting savings opportunities, and a spending breakdown chart on the right.

## Design Family

Liquid Glass

## Key Design Decisions

- Token contract enforced throughout
- No mixing with other design families
