# TradePulse — Trading Terminal — Design Session

## User Intent

I need a professional trading terminal UI. Dark monochrome, no color except green for live status. Watchlist on the left with 8 tickers, a candlestick chart in the center for BTC, order book depth on the right, and open positions at the bottom. Bloomberg-meets-Dieter-Rams aesthetic.

## Design Family

Monochrome

## Key Design Decisions

- Token contract enforced throughout
- No mixing with other design families
