# ShipTrack — Logistics Operations — Design Session

## User Intent

Build a shipment tracking operations center. Monochrome dark theme, one green accent for live status only. Sidebar with nav and metrics, main content with stats cards and a shipments table showing tracking IDs, routes, carriers, and ETAs. Carrier performance chart and exceptions list at the bottom.

## Design Family

Monochrome

## Key Design Decisions

- Token contract enforced throughout
- No mixing with other design families
